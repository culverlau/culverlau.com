Culver Lau is an entrepreneur, full-stack developer, photographer and brand consultant based in New York City. As the former Director of Website Development at Pearl Marketing, Culver worked with local New Hampshire organizations including the Greater Nashua Chamber of Commerce, United Way, and the Boys and Girls Club, among many other businesses within the Northern New England area.

Culver attended the University of California, Santa Barbara (UCSB), earning separate degrees in communication studies and biological sciences, reflecting his multi-disciplinary interests. As a collegiate rower, Culver won championships at the national and regional level, and went on to coach at various teams, including UCSB, New York Athletic Club (NYAC) and Vanderbilt University.

In addition to his experience with technology, Culver has extensive experience behind the lens. Culver's first forray in photography began as a teenager with his father's 70s Fujica film camera. Over the years, he has sought to capture scenes from across America.
